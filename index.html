About PokeMUD 0.1

What is PokeMUD?

PokeMUD is a way for multiple players to connect and play Pokemon Tabletop Adventures together online, via a simple chat interface. It is in development, and not currently functional.

PokeMUD is released under the terms of the GNU AGPL. This means that you are entitled to download and modify the program, and use it yourself, provided that you extend the same rights to your own users, under the terms of this license.

Download links will exist here at some point.

PokeMUD is based heavily off of PerlMUD, from Boutell.Com, Inc.. PerlMUD was released under the MIT license on July 4th, 2011.
